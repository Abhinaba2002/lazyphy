About my Work

I am currently engaged in the quantum simulation of condensed matter and molecular systems. I employ variational techniques and imaginary time evolution to explore the ground state and various properties of strongly correlated fermionic systems. My next objective is to extend the application of imaginary time evolution to scalable quantum circuit architectures. Additionally, I plan to integrate the concepts of quantum computation into superconducting materials, which could contribute to advanced research, potentially during my Ph.D. studies.
